2.8 Fill-In the blanks in each of the following:

a) ________ are used to document a program and improve its readability.

b) The function used to display information on the screen is ________.

c) A C statement that makes a decision is ________.

d) Calculations are normally performed by ________ statements.

e) The ________ function inputs values from the keyboard.



Answer

a) comment
b) printf
c) if 
d) assignment
e) scanf
